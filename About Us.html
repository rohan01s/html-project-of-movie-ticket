MOVIEX.com is an online movie ticketing website founded by PKY Theatres and in 2022; CBS Corporation, Famous Players, and National Amusements all came on board prior to launch; and it is now a subsidiary of PKY Media. MOVIEX.com provides movie times for all theaters, and online ticket purchasing for all Clearview Cinemas and National Amusements theaters, among other smaller chains; such as Mann Theatres in Los Angeles. In 2022, MOVIEX.com sold over 16 million tickets for over 200 exhibitors, with 14,000 screens.
